SPORTS PROGRAM

In DASH, physial health is equivalently important as the mental health.

DASH' Sports Program was created to cater the employee's sports enthusiasm. All possible indoor and outdoor sports are made possible through the creativity and efforts of the anually-elected officers of the program.


DASH Sports 2010 Tip-off

This year’s new set of dynamic sports committee for DASH Sports Program (DSP) 2010 conducted the tip-off event last March 20, 2010 at the Metro Sports Center, Lahug, Cebu City bearing the theme “Fostering camaraderie, fun and healthy living”.

The turnover of all the responsibilities from 2009 DSP Over-all Chairperson Mr. Bailey Jay Del Campo (Bailey) to his successor Mr. Aldino Dionson (Aldi) took place during the event.
DASH Sports Program 2009 Tip-Off Activity

"Promoting wellness and camaraderie through sports", displays proudly from the tarpaulin as the new theme of DASH Sports Program 2009 while enthusiastic team members of Dash Engineering Philippines Incorporated (DASH) willingly broke-off their regular Saturday routine to participate on the opening ceremony held at Metro Sports, Lahug Cebu City last February 21,2009.
