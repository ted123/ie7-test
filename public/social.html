SOCIAL GATHERINGS

DASH employees enjoy various social gatherings from group excursions, departmental parties, occasional galas to company-wide outings and celebrations.

These activities promote social interaction, relaxation and bonding among the employees


A Night to say WELCOME and THANK YOU

In its efforts to make the management system transparent to the employees, Dash Engineering Phils., Inc. held a formal Turnover ceremony as Mr. Yukihiro Mimura passes on his presidency to Mr. Takuya Aramaki at Marco Polo Hotel, Cebu City last April 30, 2010.

DASH Board of Director Mr. Yoshihiro Onuma, MES Shipbuilding’s Mr. Taiji Maeda, Mr. Yutaka Nagase and Mr. Shinobu Kinoshita graced the event along with Mr. Hitoshi Miyata and Mr. Mimura’s wife, Mrs. Chieko Mimura. An estimated 310 DASH employees were also present to witness the event.
A Holiday Soiree

Another year has come and gone and what better way to sum up the year’s events than to celebrate it with a holiday soiree. Celebrating the yuletide season “DASH-style” has always been an event every employee looks forward to.

For the third year in a row, Dash Engineering Philippines, Incorporated (DASH) celebrated the yuletide cheers at Marco Polo Plaza Hotel last December 18, 2009. DASH employees graced the halls of the famous hotel looking fabulous and stunning in their formal attires.
Breakaway to Plantation Bay

DASH Engineering had a memorable time-out event as the whole company escaped to the breathtakingly beautiful Plantation Bay Resort and Spa.

The outing literally was a whole day event in the light that the fun extended until almost midnight. All attendees took advantage of a day tour package composed of hearty buffet meals, unlimited cavorting in the wide range of water pools and lagoons, full use of amenities, and other awesome activities like bicycling, wall climbing and on top of that is the very exciting zip-lining.
