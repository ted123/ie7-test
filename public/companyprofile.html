COMPANY PROFILE

Over 20 years of existence in the field of Plant Engineering & Shipbuilding industry, Dash Engineering Philippines Incorporated (DASH) has established its identity as a trusted Engineering Design firm. With a promising performance, the company conducts businesses with international clientele in numerous countries across the globe.

Dash is the only subsidiary of Mitsui Engineering and Shipbuilding Co., Ltd. (MES) of Japan that is based in the Philippines, located at i1 Bldg., Jose Ma. Del Mar Ave., Cebu IT Park, Apas, Cebu City.

Dash works hand-in-hand with MES in providing quality services at competitive costs through creditable technology and at the same time contributing to the development of the society and individuals.
