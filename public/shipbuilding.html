SHIPBUILDING ENGINEERING

Shipbuilding Department was organized by DASH Engineering Philippines Incorporated (DASH) together with Mitsui Engineering & Shipbuilding (MES) to work with the engineering and designs of merchant bulk carrier ships, training ships, explorer and drilling ships, container carriers, fishing vessels, buoy tender vessels and other small and large non-conventional ships.

Job Scope:

Shipbuilding Department job scope is per section-based. The department currently holds six sections. Each section contributes specific outputs or services for the design of the above-mentioned ships. The following are the different sections with their corresponding outputs:

NC & Mould Loft Section
  • Reduced scale lofting of ship hull plates and shell expansions
  • Nesting of plates of hull construction drawings
  • Making of manual list of ship structures and small plates



Hull Tribon Section
  • 3D Model of ship hull structure
  • Painting surface area calculation of all ship hull plates




Hull Drawing Section
  • Creation and modifications of Yard Plans
  • Hull Stress Analysis




Piping Section
  • Layout and modification of piping diagrams
  • 3D pipe routing and arrangement design
  • 3D modeling and arrangement design of equipments
  • 3D modeling and arrangement design of penetration pieces and pipe supports


Electrical Section
  • Making and modification of ship electrical one-line diagrams
  • Layout and arrangement of ship electrical equipments
  • Making and filling of ship electrical cable lists
  • 3D modeling and layout of electrical cableways
  • Prepare and create connections of electrical system diagram through panels & equipments

Outfitting Drawing Section
  • Making and modification of Fire Control & Life Saving Plans
  • Working Instruction Drawing for Yard Use
  • 3D fittings modeling for Hull & Machinery section
  • Inventory listing
